Who We Are

We created Dish! during our online coding bootcamp through the University of Texas. Our team includes Disha, Meredith, Mitch, and Noah. We came to together to solve the problem every group of friends faces- Where should we go to eat?! Our mission is to make chosing a restaurant in you area EASY and FUN. Through our interactive platform, you will be able to work with friends to decide where your next meal should be.
